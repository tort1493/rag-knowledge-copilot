# Change Management Policy

Production changes must be deployed through the approved continuous delivery pipeline. Direct manual edits in production are prohibited except during an approved emergency change.

Standard changes require peer review and a rollback plan before deployment. Emergency changes still require retrospective documentation within two business days.

High-risk changes should be scheduled during the published maintenance window of Sundays from 1:00 AM to 3:00 AM Eastern Time.

The release manager owns the final go or no-go decision for scheduled releases.
