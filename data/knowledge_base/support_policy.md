# Customer Support Policy

The support team handles customer questions for the Atlas Knowledge Copilot product during standard coverage hours of Monday through Friday, 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM Eastern Time, excluding company holidays.

Customers can submit support requests through the support portal or by emailing support@atlas.example. Chat support is not offered for this product.

The initial response target for standard support tickets is one business day. Tickets marked urgent by an authorized customer administrator are reviewed within four business hours during support coverage hours.

Support agents may answer product usage questions, investigate reproducible bugs, and provide status updates on known incidents. They do not make billing changes directly and do not approve custom contract language.
