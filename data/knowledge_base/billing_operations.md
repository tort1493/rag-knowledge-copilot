# Billing Operations Guide

Invoices are issued annually in advance for standard subscriptions unless the order form states otherwise.

Payment terms are net 30 days. Accounts more than 45 days past due may have new feature provisioning paused until the balance is resolved.

Billing contact changes can be requested by the billing owner or an authorized procurement contact. Support agents must verify the requester identity before updating billing contacts.

Tax exemption certificates are reviewed by finance, not by support.
