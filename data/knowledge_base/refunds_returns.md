# Refunds and Returns Policy

Atlas sells annual software subscriptions. Physical goods are not shipped, so returns do not apply.

Customers may request a refund within 30 calendar days of the initial subscription start date if they have used fewer than 5,000 document queries.

Refunds are not issued for renewals, partial months, or professional services. Approved refunds are processed back to the original payment method within 10 business days.

Refund requests must be submitted by the billing owner through the billing portal. Support agents can explain the policy but cannot approve exceptions.
