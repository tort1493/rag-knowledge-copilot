# Access Management Standard

Managers must review user access for their teams every quarter. The review must confirm least-privilege access and remove stale accounts.

Privileged access requests require approval from both the requesting manager and the system owner. Temporary elevated access automatically expires after seven days unless renewed.

Contractors may be granted access only after security training is completed and a business sponsor is assigned.

Dormant accounts with no login activity for 90 days are disabled automatically.
