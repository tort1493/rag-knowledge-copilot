# Incident Response Overview

The incident response process follows four phases: identification, containment, recovery, and retrospective.

During identification, responders confirm scope, severity, and customer impact. During containment, the team limits further harm by isolating affected systems or disabling risky workflows.

Every Sev 1 or security incident requires a retrospective document within five business days after service restoration. The retrospective must include timeline, impact, root cause, mitigations, and follow-up owners.

The communications lead is responsible for publishing customer-facing incident updates during a declared incident.
